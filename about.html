About us

Welcome to Mera Bharat, a website that offers a unique virtual tour of some of India's most famous and historic monuments. Our website is designed to take you on a journey through the rich history and culture of India, without ever leaving your home. At Mera Bharat, we are passionate about preserving India's heritage and sharing it with the world. Our team of experts has carefully curated 3D models of some of India's most iconic monuments, including the Taj Mahal, Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and many others. Our 3D models are created using cutting-edge technology, including Three.js, to ensure that every detail is captured accurately. Our website is designed to be user-friendly, allowing visitors to easily navigate through the virtual tour and explore each monument from different angles. We also provide detailed information about each monument, including its history, architecture, and cultural significance. We believe that our website is a valuable resource for tourists, students, and anyone who is interested in learning more about India's rich cultural heritage. We are committed to providing the most accurate and up-to-date information about each monument, and we are constantly updating our website with new content.
Thank you for visiting Mera Bharat, and we hope that our website will inspire you to explore the beauty and history of India's monuments.
